About the Project

This project was developed during a hackathon, leveraging the methodology from the 2019 Beirle et al. study. Our team applied this approach to analyze NOx emissions from a power plant in Poland, demonstrating the model's ability to track daily emissions, calculate yearly totals, and visualize the impact of wind on emission dispersal.
